AI citation visibility measures how often ChatGPT mentions or recommends your brand when buyers ask questions in your category. When someone types "best project management software" or "what vitamins should I take" into ChatGPT, the answer is built from sources it already trusts. Your AI citation visibility score tells you how often your brand appears in those answers versus your competitors.
ChatGPT pulls from sources it encountered during training and via web search: Reddit threads, review sites, listicles, news articles, and industry publications. Brands that appear in those sources get cited. Brands that don't, don't. Covable shows you exactly which sources are driving citations in your category so you know where to focus.
Traditional SEO tools track Google search rankings. Covable tracks AI citation visibility, which is a completely separate signal. A page can rank number one on Google and never appear in a ChatGPT response. We measure what AI recommends, which is where a growing share of buyers now go first.
A citation gap is a specific URL that ChatGPT cites when recommending competitors in your category, where your brand is not mentioned. Each gap points to a real source you can get into to start appearing in AI answers alongside your competitors.
