FAQ

Questions

Which AI do you monitor right now
ChatGPT (GPT-4o) in v1. We built for depth before breadth. It's better to have reliable, accurate data from one engine than noisy coverage across many. More engines ship in v2.
How is this different from traditional SEO tools
Traditional SEO tracks Google rankings. Covable tracks AI citations: a completely separate signal. A page can rank #1 on Google and never appear in a ChatGPT response. We measure what AI recommends, which is increasingly where buyers go first.
What exactly is a citation gap
A citation gap is a specific URL that ChatGPT cites when recommending competitors in your category, but your brand isn't mentioned in it. Each gap is a concrete piece of content you can target to get cited alongside or instead of your competitor.
Does the generated content sound AI-written
Every piece is scored on four dimensions before you see it: human-sounding, platform match, natural brand mention, and on-topic. Content that doesn't pass is regenerated automatically. You only ever see the best attempt.
How quickly do score changes show up after I post content
ChatGPT's training data updates on its own cycle, typically 2 to 6 weeks after content is live and indexed. Your score updates every time you run a scan, so you'll see the delta as it happens.
