Privacy Policy

This policy describes what data Covable collects, why we collect it, and how we protect it. We keep it plain.

Who we are

Covable is an AI citation intelligence platform. We help brands understand where and how they appear in AI-generated recommendations, and generate content to improve those citations. Our service is operated by Covable ("we", "us", "our").

If you have questions about this policy, reach us at privacy@covable.ai.

What we collect

We only collect what we need to run the service.

  • Account information: your email address, name, and password (hashed) when you sign up
  • Brand data: the URLs, brand names, and competitors you add to your account
  • Usage data: pages visited, features used, scan history, and generated content within the platform
  • Payment information: handled entirely by Stripe. We never see or store full card numbers
  • Communication data: emails you send us for support or feedback
  • Technical data: IP address, browser type, device information, and cookies required for the service to function

How we use it

  • To deliver the service: run scans, calculate visibility scores, generate content, and surface results in your dashboard
  • To improve the product: understand how features are used and where we can make things better
  • To communicate with you: account notifications, scan alerts, and responses to your support requests
  • To prevent abuse: detect and block fraudulent activity, spam, or misuse of the platform
  • To comply with law: meet legal obligations where required

We do not sell your data. We do not use your data to train AI models without your explicit consent. We do not run advertising.

Third-party services

We use a small set of trusted third parties to operate Covable. Each one has their own privacy policy.

  • Stripe: payment processing
  • Supabase: database and authentication infrastructure
  • OpenAI: powering the AI scanning and generation features
  • Vercel: hosting and content delivery
  • PostHog: product analytics (anonymised where possible)

Data shared with these providers is limited to what they need to perform their specific function. We do not authorise them to use your data for any other purpose.

Data retention

We keep your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your personal data is removed within 30 days. Scan history and generated content are deleted immediately on account deletion. Anonymised, aggregated usage statistics may be retained indefinitely as they cannot be linked back to you.

Cookies

We use a minimal set of cookies:

  • Session cookies: required to keep you logged in. These expire when you close your browser or after 30 days
  • Preference cookies: remember settings like theme or dashboard layout
  • Analytics cookies: anonymised usage data via PostHog. These can be opted out of in your account settings

We do not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking pixels.

Your rights

Depending on where you are located, you may have the right to:

  • Access a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data (right to erasure)
  • Object to or restrict certain types of processing
  • Export your data in a portable format
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent

To exercise any of these rights, email us at privacy@covable.ai. We respond within 30 days.

Security

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your data: encrypted connections (TLS), hashed passwords, access controls, and regular security reviews. No system is 100% secure. If we become aware of a breach that affects your data, we will notify you promptly.

Children

Covable is not directed at anyone under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, contact us and we will delete it immediately.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the product evolves. When we make material changes, we will notify you by email or by a prominent notice in the dashboard. The date at the top of this page reflects when the policy was last updated.
